Sheep

We offer top quality hair sheep. Our animals are pasture raised and hot-wire trained. The sheep are a Dorper and St. Croix cross, they're easy to manage and parasite resistant. Perfect for any homestead or farm. We provide a self-inspection certificate for each animal.

Working Dogs

Our livestock guardian dogs are a Great Pyrenees & Anatolian Shepperd mix. Raised in the pasture with sheep from birth and from a working lineage, our dogs are extremely smart and independent. They know exactly what to do and you'll rest easier knowing they're watching after your flock.
