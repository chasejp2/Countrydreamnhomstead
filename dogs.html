Our Dogs

Livestock guardian dogs provide invaluable protection for farms and ranches by deterring predators such as coyotes, wolves, and stray dogs, reducing the need for lethal control methods. Unlike herding dogs, their primary role is to live full-time with the flock or herd, forming strong bonds with the animals they protect. This constant presence helps keep livestock calm and safe, leading to lower stress and better overall health.

Our dogs are a mix of Great Pyrenees and Anatolian Shepherd breeds, known for their protective instincts and adaptability to various environments. They are raised with our sheep from a young age, ensuring they are well-socialized and effective guardians.
